The Best Movies According to Oleksandr

My top 3 movies of all-time

Sherlock

A brilliantly written and well-acted series. Cumberbatch is a fantastic choice for Sherlock Holmes - he nailed the role. The story had me hooked from the start to the very end. Watched it more than five times and probably will do more. Highly recommend!

The Matrix

Immensely entertaining, intriguingly philosophical and just about one of the best films ever made!

Home Alone

Heartwarming Holiday family classic.
